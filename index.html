SOFTWARICA COLLEGE

Softwarica College is working in collaboration with Coventry University to offer a range of undergraduate programmes. On successful completion students will receive Coventry University degree award. An award-winning university, we are committed to providing our students with the best possible experience. We continue to invest in both our facilities and our innovative approach to education. Our students benefit from industry-relevant teaching, and resources and support designed to help them succeed. These range from our modern library and computing facilities to dedicated careers advice and our impressive Students’ Union activities.
